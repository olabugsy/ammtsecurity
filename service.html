At Amt Security Inc. our security guard services provide a unique blend of security expertise and exceptional customer service, offering a comprehensive solution for safeguarding premises while enhancing the overall experience for visitors, residents, and clients. These highly-trained professionals serve as both protectors and hospitality ambassadors, ensuring safety and creating a positive environment. Here's a concise overview of the key aspects and services offered :

Emergency Response

Trained to handle emergencies, Amt security guards can respond to incidents such as medical emergencies, fire alarms, and evacuation procedures, providing essential support until specialized help arrives.

Security and Surveillance

Our Guards at Amt Security Inc. maintain a vigilant watch over the premises, utilizing surveillance technology and physical patrols to detect and deter security threats.

Crisis Management

In the event of a crisis, security guards collaborate with emergency services and internal teams to manage the situation, maintain order, and ensure the safety of all individuals involved.

Event Security

For public events, gatherings, and exhibitions our security guards provide crowd management, access control, and overall safety coordination.

Access Control

Unarmed security guards manage entry and exit points, ensuring that only authorized individuals gain access to the premises. They check identification, verify credentials, and maintain visitor logs.

Loss Prevention

Unarmed security guards help prevent theft, shoplifting, and other forms of loss by monitoring surveillance cameras, conducting bag checks, and maintaining a visible presence in retail settings.

Training and Expertise

Unarmed security guards undergo rigorous training programs that cover areas such as security procedures, communication skills, conflict resolution, first aid, and legal regulations. Their expertise ensures effective and responsible security practices.
